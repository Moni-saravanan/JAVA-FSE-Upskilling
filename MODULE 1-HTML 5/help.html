Help & Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event?

Go to the "Events" section on the home page, fill in the registration form, and click submit. A confirmation message will appear once it is successful.

Can I update my preferences?

Yes! Your selected event type is saved for your next visit. You can clear or change it using the “Clear Preferences” button in the form section.

How do I find nearby events?

Use the “Find Nearby Events” button. The site will request your location and display coordinates or suggest locations near you.

Why do I see a warning when leaving the page?

That’s a safety feature to prevent accidental loss of form data. It shows if you try to leave a form page before submitting.
